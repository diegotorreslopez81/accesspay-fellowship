# Roadmap

Twelve-month plan for AccessPay. Paired with [proposal.md](proposal.md). The three tracks (research, technical, advocacy) run in parallel, not sequential.

## Shape

Every month ends with a public artefact: a tag, a progress note, a publication, a PR, or a report. The cadence is what makes the work visible and keeps me honest.

## Month-by-month

### Month 1. Framing

- **Research:** literature review, draft interview guide, ethics and consent templates, pre-registered study design on the repo.
- **Technical:** bootstrap `openpay-impact` shared module, Rafiki sandbox local, first stub integration in Contestia (behind flag, off).
- **Advocacy:** first monthly progress note in #cfp-sdk. FOSDEM CFP research.
- **Public output:** repo tag `v0.1`, first monthly post.

### Month 2. First wave prep

- **Research:** shortlist finalised for wave 1 (5 entities), outreach sent, first 2 interviews scheduled.
- **Technical:** Contestia flow selected (candidate flow based on first research signals). Wallet provider shortlist for staging.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post.
- **Public output:** `v0.2` tag with wave 1 plan public, interview guide v1.0.

### Month 3. Fieldwork wave 1 begins

- **Research:** interviews 1, 2, 3 of 5 done. Raw transcripts.
- **Technical:** Contestia integration reaches end-to-end in the sandbox.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post.

### Month 4. Wave 1 ends, first analysis

- **Research:** interviews 4, 5 done. Analysis of wave 1, first case sketches drafted. Member-checking with interviewees.
- **Technical:** first pilot clinic opt-in for Contestia integration, real-money staging test.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post, Progress Report due.
- **Public output:** wave 1 case sketches drafted (not yet public), progress report to the Foundation.

### Month 5. Wave 2 begins, Contestia live

- **Research:** wave 2 outreach, 2 interviews done, guide refined based on wave 1 learnings.
- **Technical:** Contestia integration live in one pilot clinic. First real Open Payments transactions in production.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post. OSSEU CFP submitted.

### Month 6. Contestia post-mortem

- **Research:** wave 2 interviews 3, 4, 5 done.
- **Technical:** Contestia post-mortem written and published. Talk 2 draft based on it.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post, Talk 2 submitted to OSSEU 2026.
- **Public output:** Contestia integration post-mortem published.

### Month 7. Wave 3 prep, AGING-AI begins

- **Research:** wave 2 analysis, refined research framework. Wave 3 outreach targeting pan-European entities.
- **Technical:** AGING-AI integration scoping with DKV pilot team. First proof-of-concept subsidy flow against sandbox.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post. FOSDEM 2027 CFP submitted.

### Month 8. Wave 3 fieldwork

- **Research:** 3 of 5 wave 3 interviews done.
- **Technical:** AGING-AI PoC end-to-end in sandbox.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post. OSSEU talk delivered if accepted.

### Month 9. Saturation check, AGING-AI staging

- **Research:** wave 3 complete. Saturation assessment, decide whether to extend by 1 to 2 interviews. Cross-case analysis begins.
- **Technical:** AGING-AI integration staged with one real elderly test user.
- **Advocacy:** monthly post.

### Month 10. Drafting

- **Research:** findings paper first full draft. Peer review requests to 2 to 3 Interledger reviewers and 2 to 3 third-sector reviewers.
- **Technical:** AGING-AI real-world test, document findings on what's ready and what isn't.
- **Advocacy:** practitioner's guide drafted.
- **Public output:** findings paper draft shared with reviewers.

### Month 11. Refining

- **Research:** findings paper v2 based on reviewer feedback. Spanish translation.
- **Technical:** AGING-AI final documentation. `openpay-impact` module v1.0.0.
- **Advocacy:** practitioner's guide polished, English and Spanish versions.
- **Public output:** findings paper ready for publication, `openpay-impact` 1.0.0.

### Month 12. Publish and close out

- **Research:** findings paper published (CC BY-SA), both languages. Case sketches publicly archived.
- **Technical:** upstream contributions filed, repo tagged v1.0.0.
- **Advocacy:** FOSDEM talk delivered (if accepted) or recorded as a fallback session for the Interledger community. Practitioner's guide published.
- **Public output:** final report to the Foundation 30 days after project end.

| Buffer | $1,000 | Unexpected needs. |

## Reporting cadence

- **Monthly** short public post (Interledger community forum or #cfp-sdk) with the month's outputs and next-month plan.
- **Monthly** brief update to the Fellowship Program Officer, same content reformatted.
- **Quarterly** (months 3, 6, 9) slightly longer check-in, pace and risks review.
- **Final report** 30 days after month 12 close.

## Public-first

The repo is public from day one. All commits, proposal drafts, research notes, technical work, and advocacy materials are in the open. Every upstream PR is disclosed per Badge AI conventions. This aligns with the signal Jeremiah raised on 3 April 2026 about keeping ecosystem conversations in observable channels.
